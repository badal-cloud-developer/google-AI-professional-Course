# Ways to Write a Best Prompt

## The 4 Key Elements of a Great Prompt

| Element | What it means |
|---------|--------------|
| **Persona** | Who the AI should act as |
| **Task** | What you want the AI to do |
| **Format** | How you want the output (points, table, paragraph) |
| **Context** | Background details that shape the answer |

---

## Example Prompt (All 4 Elements Combined)

```
You are a freelancer from Nepal who has been doing freelancing in 
data analytics for the past 5 years.

Give advice to a fresher data analyst from Nepal who wants to start 
their freelancing journey and grab small projects.

The fresher has:
- Built guided/practice projects on data analytics
- No real world work experience
- Currently doing an internship

Give the advice in point format.
```

---

## Breakdown of the Example Prompt

**🧑 Persona:**
> You are a freelancer from Nepal with 5 years of data analytics
> freelancing experience.

**✅ Task:**
> Give advice to a fresher who wants to start freelancing and
> grab small projects.

**📋 Format:**
> Point format.

**📌 Context:**
> - Fresher is from Nepal
> - Has guided/practice projects but no real world experience
> - Currently doing an internship

---

## What I Learned

- Adding **persona** makes the AI respond from a realistic perspective
- Adding **context** about the fresher made the advice more specific
  and relevant
- **Format** keeps the output clean and easy to read
- All 4 elements together = a much more useful and targeted response

---

## Key Takeaway

> The more specific your prompt, the more useful the output.
> Persona + Task + Format + Context is the winning formula.
